# Certification Comparison Table

*All 19 certifications covered in this guide, side by side — structural comparison, not exact current pricing.*

Exact prices, question counts, and exam lengths change over time and vary by region and promotion. This table is built to be useful even after those numbers drift — the structural facts (who issues it, which role it's for, whether a course is mandatory, whether it expires) are stable; anywhere a specific figure is genuinely uncertain, it's marked for you to verify directly with the issuing body rather than stated as a fact that might be stale by the time you read it.

## Scrum Master track

| Certification | Issuing Body | Prerequisite | Format | Renewal | Best For |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| [PSM I](scrum-org/psm-i.md) | Scrum.org | None. No mandatory course. | Self-study, open-book online exam; ~80 questions / ~60 minutes, ~85% to pass — verify current details on scrum.org. | None — never expires. | Anyone proving foundational Scrum knowledge fast and cheaply. The default first cert for developers moving into the SM role. |
| [PSM II](scrum-org/psm-ii.md) | Scrum.org | None enforced; PSM I-equivalent knowledge plus real SM experience is the honest floor. | Self-study, open-book, denser scenario questions; ~30 questions / ~90 minutes — verify on scrum.org. | None — never expires. | Practicing Scrum Masters proving applied, scenario-level judgment beyond rule recall. |
| [PSM III](scrum-org/psm-iii.md) | Scrum.org | None enforced; years of varied real-world SM experience is the realistic floor. | Self-study, open-book, essay-style scenario questions evaluated by a human — verify format/pass mark on scrum.org. | None — never expires. | Experienced Scrum Masters seeking the highest expert-tier proof of judgment. A capstone, not an early-career goal. |
| [CSM](scrum-alliance/csm.md) | Scrum Alliance | None, but a mandatory ~2-day course by a Certified Scrum Trainer, with exam eligibility only after. | Mandatory paid course + a comparatively easier follow-on exam. | Expires; SEUs + renewal fee on a recurring cycle. | Employer-funded training and the most broadly recognized entry-level name-brand credential. |
| [A-CSM](scrum-alliance/a-csm.md) | Scrum Alliance | Active CSM + minimum real SM experience (verify on scrumalliance.org). | Mandatory advanced course + assessment. | Expires; SEUs + fee, same family as CSM. | Practicing SMs on the Alliance track wanting mid-tier depth beyond CSM. |
| [CSP-SM](scrum-alliance/csp-sm.md) | Scrum Alliance | Active A-CSM + substantial real-world experience. | Application-and-experience-based, not a single exam. | Expires; SEUs + fee. | The Alliance track's capstone — experienced practitioners who've completed CSM → A-CSM → CSP-SM. |
| [SAFe Scrum Master](safe/safe-scrum-master.md) | Scaled Agile, Inc. | None, but a mandatory ~2-day instructor-led course. | Mandatory course + exam via Scaled Agile's platform. | Annual renewal to stay "active" — verify on scaledagile.com. | SMs working inside an organization running SAFe — largely non-optional there. |
| [SAFe Advanced Scrum Master](safe/safe-advanced-scrum-master.md) | Scaled Agile, Inc. | Generally expects prior SAFe SM + real ART experience. | Mandatory course + exam, deeper ART-level content. | Annual renewal, same family as SAFe SM. | SMs advancing into senior/ART-level facilitation roles in an established SAFe org. |

## Reading the table

- **Format column** is the fastest way to see the core philosophical split covered in [which certification should I get](00-which-certification-should-i-get.md): every Scrum.org credential, including PSD, is a self-study exam with no mandatory course; every Scrum Alliance and SAFe credential requires a paid, instructor-led course as a gate before you can even sit the assessment.
- **Renewal** is the other structural divide: Scrum.org credentials are earned once and never expire; Scrum Alliance and SAFe credentials require ongoing renewal (SEUs and fees, or annual terms) to stay "current."
- Treat every specific number in this table as approximate and **verify current details on the issuing body's own site before registering or budgeting**.

For the full reasoning behind which of these fits your specific role and situation, see [which certification should I get](00-which-certification-should-i-get.md). For how the underlying skill most of these certifications test is taught in this guide regardless of track, see [Professional Scrum Competencies](../03-professional-scrum-competencies/00-overview.md).
